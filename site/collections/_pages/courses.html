I offer personalized Spanish and English instruction for professionals, translation students, and language learners. Each course is designed to adapt to your specific context, pace, and goals.

Available Courses

Spanish for Professionals — Focus on business communication, cultural nuances, and industry-specific terminology. Ideal for professionals working with Spanish-speaking markets.

English for Spanish Speakers — Comprehensive English instruction with emphasis on professional communication, academic writing, and cultural context.

Translation Studies — Advanced training for translation students covering theory, practice, and specialized domains (legal, technical, literary).

Customized Corporate Training — Tailored programs for teams and organizations needing language skills for specific projects or markets.

Teaching Approach

With 17 years of teaching experience across three continents, I bring a pedagogical background that shapes everything I do. I think about how meaning lands, not just how it translates. My courses emphasize:

  • Practical, real-world application
  • Cultural competence and context
  • Interactive, student-centered learning
  • Flexible scheduling for busy professionals

Pricing & Format

Courses are offered online via video conferencing. Individual and small group sessions available. Contact me for current rates and availability.
